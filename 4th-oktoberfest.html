4th Annual Honolulu Oktoberfest

Oktoberfest is quickly approaching and we are very excited. German food, entertainment and of course... lots of beer. There will be 20+ beers to enjoy from all over the world. Get your Limited Edition Eat The Street Honolulu Oktoberfest Stein here.

This is the first ever 2 day Eat The Street. Day 1 will be the family friendly and food frenzy day of the event. Vendors will be serving at least 1 German themed dish. And for Oktoberfest our beer garden will be bigger and better, with 11 beers to choose from on Friday night.

Day 2

We are coming back on Saturday for day 2 of Eat The Street Honolulu Oktoberfest to the same location. Day 2 will be a 21+ only Eat The Street Honolulu Oktoberfest with less food trucks and more beer. We will be adding 10 additional Oktoberfest beers to the beer garden for a total of 21 beers to explore on Saturday.
